Aim : To Help India fight Depression and Suicides

What Statistics day about Depression

Depression has become a common problem. With the advancement in technology, there are increase in number of suicides because of depression. Stats say 56,675,959 people suffered from depression as for 2015(source WHO). 42.5% of corporate Employs suffer from depression.Govt says 1 in 5 people in India needs psychatric orpsychological. about 371 sucides happen daily in India.

What are our plans

According to studies, social media activity of an individual can used to predict the tendency of a person to be in depression. We will ask users to login through facebook accounts. Now we can use deep learning trained algorithm to analyse user activity and engagements to predict the tendency of a person to be suffering from depression. If we realise that the tendency is very high , we can contact his family/friends to tell them that this person has high chances ot be in depression and he needs some one to connect to.

We publish articles from experienced pyshologists so that people can actully figure out how to cope up with depression and how to help others in such state.
